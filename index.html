How to use FollowOp
Getting Started
Adding FollowOp to your home screen
On Android: open followop.app in Chrome → tap the three dots menu → tap "Add to Home Screen". On iPhone: open in Safari → tap the share button → tap "Add to Home Screen".
Free vs Pro
Free gives you 50 contacts, 10 events, and 5 Read The Room reads per month. Pro adds client management, professional reports, and unlimited everything. Try Pro free for 14 days.
Capturing Contacts
⚡ Hot Intro — full capture
Tap Hot Intro in the header or dashboard. Scan their physical card, digital card, or QR code — AI reads it and fills the form. Add their photo, notes, and which client they connect to.
⚡ Quick Capture — fast mode
At a busy event when you only have 30 seconds. Tap Quick Capture — just name and phone/email. You can complete their profile later.
The 4 contact types
⚡ Hot Intro Connect them with someone right now — urgent.
📌 Follow Up Reach out yourself this week.
💡 Recommend Great for a client or colleague.
🔍 Intel Good to know — keep in your network.
Scanning tips
For physical cards — good lighting, card flat and fully visible. For QR codes — screenshot the full profile page rather than scanning live. For Read The Room — any profile photo, LinkedIn screenshot, website about page, or bio works.
Read The Room
What it does
Upload any image of someone — their LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, website, bio, event program, or even a photo of them — and AI analyzes their communication personality and tells you exactly how to follow up.
The 4 communication types
Results Driver, Relationship Builder, Detail Architect, Vision Seeker. Each one gets a specific follow-up approach and a suggested message you can copy and send.
Events
Log an event before you walk in
Tap Log Event and fill in the event name, date, and which client you're representing. Then as you capture contacts at the event they'll be linked to it automatically.
Event ROI score
After logging an event FollowOp calculates your efficiency — contacts captured divided by conversations had. Use this to decide which events to repeat.
Clients — Pro Only
Adding a client
Tap the Clients tab → Add. Fill in their brand name, your elevator pitch for them, what events and contacts matter, and what not to say. This becomes your reference in the room.
Social links
Add their social media handles in their client profile. At events you can open their profiles instantly and tag them in posts without searching.
Reports — Pro Only
Weekly report
Fill in your week's activity — events, conversations, contacts, intros, key people, community intel. Tap Generate and a professional report copies to your clipboard. Paste into email and send to your client.
Monthly summary
Same as weekly but covers the full month. Includes recommended events for next month and goals. Send at the end of each month.
Your Data
Where is my data stored?
Your data is stored on your device in your browser. It does not sync to a server yet. Export regularly to back up your contacts.
Exporting contacts
Go to Settings → Export Contacts to CSV. Downloads a spreadsheet with all your contact info and notes. Send to your VA or open in Google Sheets.
Saving to your phone contacts
Open any contact → tap Save to Phone Contacts. Downloads a vCard file that opens in your phone's native contacts app.
